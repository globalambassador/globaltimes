UPDATE

A group of high school students will be marching to the Metropolitan Detention Center in downtown LA for a rally and press conference in support of immigrant rights. The students are calling for Congress to pass legislation to permanently end family separations and establish alternatives to detention. In addition, they are calling for improved enforcement of CA immigrant labor laws. Join us.

  • Title of Action: College, Not Cages
  • Date: Wednesday, May 22nd
  • Location: Metropolitan Detention Center in downtown LA (535 Alameda St, Los Angeles, CA 90012)
  • Time: 4pm - rally and press conference at Federal Detention Center
