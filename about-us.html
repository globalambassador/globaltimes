About Us

Welcome! We are a group of students from the Los Angeles School of Global Studies (LASGS) who are dedicated to become young advocates. We use our college and career pathway of ¨Design, Media, and Social Change¨ to stand up to injustices. We collaborate with the Constitutional Rights Foundation to focus on key issues that are affecting not only us students but the community around us. We have been working on our Civil Action Project (CAP) since December of 2018 and focusing on issues about immigration and the implementations of health classes in LAUSD. On May 20th students from LASGS will be visiting Sacramento to take part in rallying and make legislative visits about immigrant workers rights and expanding medical healthcare to undocumented immigrants.
